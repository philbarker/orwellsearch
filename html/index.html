Orwell Search

Search works by and about George Orwell. Try, for example, tea or Wigan.

After the initial search you will be able to refine your search by source, e.g. Orwell's diaries or a specific novel. Do try that, for reasons I really don't understand, sometimes narrowing on a specific source increases the number of results. (Google foo, go figure!?)

Sources

This Google custom search engine searches the following sources of works by and about George Orwell:

  • Part of the Orwell Foundation Resources which includes some essays and shorter pieces by Orwell, Orwell's diaries, and many essays about Orwell.
  • English Wikipedia articles about "George Orwell" and his works
  • Full text of books and essays by Orwell from Project Gutenberg Australia (they are out of copyright in Australia, but may not be where you are).
